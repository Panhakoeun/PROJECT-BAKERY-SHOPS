Ladurée

Ladurée is a historic French luxury patisserie founded in 1862, famous worldwide for its iconic macarons and other gourmet pastries and desserts.
The company offers a range of products, including sweets, cakes, teas, jams, and gifts, and provides unique dining experiences through its tea salons and restaurants.
You can purchase Ladurée products online or through their numerous global locations for home delivery, local pickup, or in-store shopping.
